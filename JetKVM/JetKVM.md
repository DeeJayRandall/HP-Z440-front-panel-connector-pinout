To connect a JetKVM ATX Module to the HP Z440 while keeping
the HP front panel controls functional, create a 26-pin flat
ribbon cable y-splitter. This permits connecting the JetKVM
breakout ribbon to the correct pins while also plugging the
original HP female front panel connector into the 26-pin
cable extension. Remove the #6 key pin from the end of the
26-pin ribbon cable to permit the connection.
